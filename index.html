Hello, my name is Megan Flood and I love working with computers. Though I have a degree in Chemistry from Reed College, I wrote my thesis on the computer modeling of a reaction, no physical chemicals involved! I have been working to expand my knowledge of computer programming, which has so far been limited to some very specialized programs. As such, I have already taken several classes in Python and Java, and am currently taking classes in JavaScript.

I also have a deep interest in art and love of creativity. I have experience working in HTML and CSS from a class in Web Design and I am excited to combine this experience with the JavaScript I am currently learning.

Outside of messing about on computers, I also enjoy doing art, playing video games, and reading science-fiction and fantasy, especially the Diskworld novels of Terry Pratchett.
